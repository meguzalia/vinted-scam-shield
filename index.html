FAQ

Common questions

How do I spot fake vintage items on Vinted?
Real vintage items have specific brand history and authentic wear patterns. Red flags include: modern fast fashion brands (Shein, Zara, H&M) labelled as "vintage" or "Y2K", no clothing tag photo, vague descriptions with no mention of where or when the item was bought. Vinted Scam Shield automatically detects when a listing claims vintage status for a brand that isn't genuinely vintage.
How can I detect Shein or Temu resellers on Vinted?
Shein/Temu resellers typically have: the "Frequent Uploads" badge, very few genuine reviews, no real brand listed or a made-up brand name, template descriptions with template phrases like "never worn" or "carefully packaged and shipped", and 6+ hashtags. Our extension automatically flags this combination — and the built-in Google Lens button lets you reverse-search photos to find the original listing on Shein.
Does Vinted buyer protection actually work?
Vinted's buyer protection covers you if an item doesn't arrive or is significantly not as described — but only if you pay through Vinted and raise a dispute within the window. In practice, many buyers report that disputes can be challenging to resolve, even with evidence. The best protection is avoiding suspicious purchases before you make them — which is exactly what this extension helps with.
How do I check if Vinted photos are stolen or AI-generated?
Use reverse image search — our extension has a built-in Google Lens button on every listing. Click it to instantly check if the photo appears on Shein, Temu, or AliExpress. For AI-generated model photos (created by tools like VintDress), look for: suspiciously perfect lighting on a second-hand item, professional model poses from a new seller, or photos that look like fashion website product shots rather than someone's wardrobe.
Is the extension really free? What's the catch?
Completely free, no catch. No account required, no data collected, no ads. Everything runs locally in your browser. The code is open source on GitHub so you can verify it yourself. A future paid tier with AI-powered analysis is planned — but the core protection will always be free.

Works on all regional Vinted domains: vinted.at, vinted.de, vinted.fr, vinted.co.uk, vinted.it, vinted.es, vinted.nl, vinted.be, vinted.pl and vinted.com.
